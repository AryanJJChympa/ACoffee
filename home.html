Frequently Asked Questions

What beans do you use for brewing?

We use 100% organic Arabica beans handpicked from certified farms around the world. Our beans are carefully selected for their exceptional quality and unique flavor profiles.

Can I place an order for bulk corporate events?

Yes! We cater for events and offer custom packages for corporate orders. Contact us for special pricing and arrangements for your business needs.

Do you have vegan or dairy-free options?

Absolutely! We offer oat milk, almond milk, and coconut milk options. All our dairy-free alternatives are carefully selected to complement our coffee perfectly.
